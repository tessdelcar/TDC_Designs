Tess del Carmen

Graphic Designer | Web Developer

Tess 'Teresa' del Carmen resides in her beautiful hometown Miami, FL. Tess has many interests but her favorites are anything that spark 💡 her creativity. Presently, she is a student at F.I.R.S.T. Institute’s Graphic Design and Web Development Program set to graduate in the fall of 2022. She is as confident as she is passionate in her skill set in Adobe programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, and Xd. Since starting at F.I.R.S.T. Tess' bread and butter has become branding, photo retouching, and front-end web development.
